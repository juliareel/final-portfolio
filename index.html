About Me

Hello! My name is Julia, and I am a third-year undergraduate student at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, pursuing a Bachelor's of Science in Information (BSI) degree. I am studying at the School of Information with a concentration on Information Analysis.

I am a highly analytical individual who loves finding patterns and connecting the dots within seas of information. I am passionate about using technology and data to gather insights and develop solutions to today's complex problems. Throughout my studies at the University of Michigan, I have acquired numerous skills in a range of technologies including Python, C++, HTML/CSS, and R. This upcoming summer, in 2022, I am excited to join Anthem Inc. Insurance as a Data Analytics Intern in the Pharmacy Operations department. I look forward to the opportunity to learn and develop my skills in data analytics in a professional environment.

Education

I grew up in Madison, Connecticut where I attended Daniel Hand High School. I graduated in June, 2019 with an unweighted GPA of 3.9/4.0. Throughout my time in high school, I was very involved in my community and spent my afternoons tutoring young students in nearby New Haven, CT. I also co-directed and individually led local blood drives with the American Red Cross, organizing drives both within DHHS and off campus.

In September, 2019, I began my studies in Ann Arbor at the University of Michigan. Earlier this year, in 2021, I was admitted to the university's upper-level program within the School of Information where I currently study Information Analysis. I expect to graduate with my Bachelor of Science in 2023. Outside of the classroom, I participate in Dance Marathon at UM, and I am a member of our campus's Girls Who Code club. I am also very involved in my sorority on campus, Delta Delta Delta.
